I've put together a selection of interesting YouTube channels I'm subscribed to. Not all of them are here, either because I don't consider them relevant or because they haven't posted anything in a long time.

## Science and Popular Science:

- [QuantumFracture](https://www.youtube.com/channel/UCbdSYaPD-lr1kW27UJuk8Pw)
  Science! And with animations! The craziest (and realest) side of the Universe.
- [Ciencia de sofá](https://www.youtube.com/channel/UCMbQbVilo-nezMvwf1BZfAA)
  Science outreach for distracted minds.
- [Jaime Altozano](https://www.youtube.com/channel/UCa3DVlGH2_QhvwuWlPa6MDQ)
  Everything about music. How it works, why it is the way it is. A channel to understand what you hear.
- [Antroporama](https://www.youtube.com/channel/UCGKzjVZGdJ0YmUqg42xfO5w)
  Science outreach about the Human Being, our behavior, mind, and our best-kept secret: the brain.
- [La gata de Schrödinger](https://www.youtube.com/channel/UCoXtmmnLCbXDiSo8GxsmOzA)
  Science outreach channel from a skeptical perspective and with a touch of humor.
- [Minuto de física](https://www.youtube.com/channel/UCMnPZh6PyA5PSYoNt0cjuxg)
  In a nutshell: cool physics and interesting science.
- [Deborah Ciencia](https://www.youtube.com/channel/UCibUX4QoSrRwmBZf0Ig-OCg)
  Chemist, science communicator, and contemporary art specialist.
- [Vsauce](https://www.youtube.com/channel/UC6nSFpj9HTCZ5t-N3Rm3-HA) _ENG_
  Science outreach and a curious spirit.
- [Carolina Jiménez](https://www.youtube.com/channel/UCq3HgwV38l3WCHx8nyFtc6A)
  VFX artist specializing in Layout for international cinema.
- [Physics Girl](https://www.youtube.com/channel/UC7DdEm33SyaTDtWYGO2CwdA) _ENG_
  Applied science.
- [Date un voltio](https://www.youtube.com/channel/UCns-8DssCBba7M4nu7wk7Aw/videos) and [Date un vlog](https://www.youtube.com/channel/UCQX_MZRCaluNKxkywkLEgfA)
  Physics outreach by Javier Santaolalla.
