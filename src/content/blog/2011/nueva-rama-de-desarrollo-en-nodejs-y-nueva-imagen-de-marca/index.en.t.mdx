**Node.js**, which we have already discussed in previous posts, has just released a new development branch, 0.5.x, to continue growing. There are numerous changes and bugfixes that you can find [here](https://github.com/joyent/node/wiki/ChangeLog). The stable branch remains 0.4.x. This is an important moment for the project as it continues its development. Evidence of this is Microsoft's support in creating a *port* for the [Windows platform](http://blog.nodejs.org/2011/06/23/porting-node-to-windows-with-microsoft%e2%80%99s-help/). They have also taken the opportunity to evolve the brand and create a [new corporate identity](http://blog.nodejs.org/2011/07/11/evolving-the-node-js-brand/) that is more consistent and identifiable.
