A couple of years ago, overcoming my shyness, I started giving <s>boring lectures</s> technical talks in public. Obviously, the first one was a disaster :sweat:, but that's what the first one is for—to learn—and also the second and the third... :joy:

Sharing knowledge this way is something I recommend for many reasons, even "selfish" ones, like the fact that it forces you to put in the effort to prepare the topic you're going to talk about.

There is also the _feedback_ you receive from the attendees, which in many cases has opened up other perspectives or threads for me to follow.

I wanted to take advantage of these relatively quiet days to compile the talks I've given and the materials I used.
