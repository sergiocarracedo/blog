The social networks we use regularly: **Facebook**, **Twitter**, **Foursquare**, **instagram**, etc., are, after all, a kind of diary of ourselves—of what we do, where we have been, the photos we have taken, etc. **[Timehop](http://timehop.com/)** is a tool that, once logged into the various networks, will send us a daily email (or we can access their website to see it) with a summary of the posts we published on social networks exactly one year ago. Very interesting for reminiscing about past events.

On the other hand, we have **[Memolane](http://memolane.com/)**, much more powerful than **Timehop**; in it, we can add many more networks in addition to those mentioned above: **Picassa, Facebook Pages, fickr, google+, youtube, etc...** The content from these networks will be displayed on a timeline, as shown in the image, through which we can travel to a specific date and remember what we 'did' that day.
